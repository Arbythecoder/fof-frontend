Tropical Smoothie Bowl

A refreshing blend of tropical fruits with coconut and granola

Prep Time

15 minutes

Servings

2 people

Category

Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mango
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Instructions

1

Combine mango, banana, and coconut milk in a blender. Blend until smooth.

2

Pour the smoothie into a bowl.

3

Top with granola, chia seeds, and additional fruit if desired.

Chef's Notes

For a thicker consistency, use frozen banana slices. You can substitute almond milk for coconut milk if preferred.
